ABOUT US

Learn About Ptarmigan.

Bringing your ideas into reality

Ptarmigan is a custom millwork and renovation company. We specialize in custom high quality hardwood furniture and residential space solutions. Everything we make is designed and made in Edmonton from sustainably sourced materials and put together using timeless hand-craft techniques.

Our Mission

Ptarmigan wishes to emphasize its craftsmanship and process in creating luxurious wood spaces and furniture. Our goal is to create a vision for our clients that is unique to their space. We do a high-quality service that is well-thought-out in terms of process and execution. We want to create pieces that will withstand the test of time and act as a statement bringing your home to life.
